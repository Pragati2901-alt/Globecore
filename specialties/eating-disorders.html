Eating disorders are serious, life-threatening conditions that require specialized, compassionate care. Our clinicians use evidence-based models including CBT-E, DBT, and family-based therapy to support recovery.

Recovery from eating disorders is possible. We provide a non-diet, weight-inclusive approach that addresses underlying trauma, perfectionism, and emotional dysregulation alongside nutritional rehabilitation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Eating Disorders therapy highly effective?
Yes. Clinical efficacy for Eating Disorders treatment is exceedingly high when utilizing modern, evidence-based practices and targeted psychiatric evaluations.

What causes Eating Disorders?
The manifestation of Eating Disorders is frequently multi-factorial, stemming from a combination of neurobiological predispositions, environmental stressors, and trauma. Our initial assessment comprehensively dissects these variables.

How do you structure therapy for Eating Disorders?
Treatment is highly personalized. We formulate a structured intervention plan initially focusing on symptom relief, transitioning subsequently to underlying cause resolution and long-term relapse prevention strategies for Eating Disorders.

Can Eating Disorders be managed without medication?
While medication can be a potent adjunctive treatment, many individuals successfully manage Eating Disorders using behavioral interventions and psychotherapy alone. Our team will collaborate with you to determine the safest, most effective route.

How soon can I schedule an appointment for Eating Disorders therapy in Atlanta?
GlobeCoRe prioritizes timely access to care. New patients seeking help for Eating Disorders are typically scheduled within a week. Contact our intake coordinators to secure the earliest available opening.
